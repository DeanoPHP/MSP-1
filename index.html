Located in the dynamic heart of Bristol, Gold's Gym stands as a bastion of strength and determination for the dedicated bodybuilder. Established in 2015 by world-renowned bodybuilder Dean Manning, our gym is not just a facilityâ€”it's the breeding ground for champions.

The Foundation of Gold's Gym

Dean Manning, a multiple-time national bodybuilding champion, founded Gold's Gym after realizing the need for a training environment that catered specifically to the serious bodybuilder. Frustrated by mainstream gyms lacking the equipment and atmosphere necessary to foster serious growth, Dean created Gold's Gym as a haven where competitive bodybuilders could thrive.
